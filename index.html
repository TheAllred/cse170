Allred’s User Manual

STYLE:

Y leadership. Peer led with high respect and high autonomy. Psychological Safety Stage 4.

Values:

Respect, accountability, and self-motivation. I value high ownership mentality, high respect for the people around you, and I value people who have the drive to accomplish difficult things.

What I don’t have patience for:

Laziness, lack of effort, and shirking responsibility. Effort has different results depending on the person, but the amount of effort given is entirely dependent on the individual. Not giving full effort is a choice and one that I cannot tolerate.

Best way to communicate with me:

Quickly, clearly, and respectfully. If my fly is down, tell me. Yes, I’ll be embarrassed. Yes, I’ll be frustrated, but if you correct me or must communicate with me, do it quickly and don’t beat around the bush.

Best way to help me:

Correct me when I make mistakes. I’m oblivious to much of the things I do. If I am struggling, chance is that I can’t see what is causing me to struggle. Do for me what I can’t do for myself and point out the things that I don’t see.

Common Misconceptions:

I am a positive person and I try to keep a high level of confidence in my abilities. That confidence often comes across as arrogance and pride. I don’t want to come across as an arrogant prick. I don’t have a know-it-all mind set. I have a learn-it-all attitude that if I don’t know something, I am confident that I can learn it.

FAULTS/Shortcomings:

Impatient with redundancy and shortcomings with myself. I want to make things better and I can often become hyper focused on a specific task that I will make mistakes in other areas from just not being aware.

Personal Compass

Workview

Right now, the main motivator for me to work is to gain experience. I am in an incredible position to learn and develop skills in a very real environment with highly skilled people. We have a mission that I believe in and can fully support. My work also pays me well. I have the money to eat well and keep my body healthy. The money also allows me to go out and have fun on activities with friends and date. My work has the potential to impact lives depending on the company that I work for. My code helps people learn about themselves and their beliefs.

Lifeview

I am here to grow and gain experience, talents, and characteristics like those of God. I want to be able to start and provide for a family. I want to be able to raise children in a loving home where they can grow to be self reliant temporally and spiritually. As I progress through life, I can help others along the way. I can mentor others on developing those skills and attributes and I can be coached in return on the skills that I lack. I am here to add to the good in the world. I can connect people together and help them see who they can become. Evil is what breaks people apart. My lifeview and work view complement each other. My work is what enables me to live the life that I want.
